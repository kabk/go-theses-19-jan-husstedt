ABSTRACT

The Plutonics Committee strongly urges the field of graphic design, to mingle and ally with other fields of knowledge. A global trauma is going on, channeling political extremes dividing humankind in two camps. Tapping on the cognitive abilities, the narratives of our world should help us in times of trouble. Help us growing greater than we are. The materials to work with, are those that mingle the past and the possible future into our present. [1]. You will find them in the alchemist’s archive, a common ground encouraging us to wonder, question and create. Change is the only constant.

Anthropocene begins with the cognitive revolution during which we gained remarkable abilities. Above all: to communicate, to use language, to create and spread fictions. We don’t really know what made us gain those abilities. What did we pick up 70.000 years ago? Did the idea of human come from some inhuman entity? Did something crash onto earth making us believe in what or who we are? Or did the idea of the human come from itself? Where is the evidence?
